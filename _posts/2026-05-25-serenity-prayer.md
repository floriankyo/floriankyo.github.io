<div class="post-lang-content" data-post-lang="en" markdown="1">

> God, give us grace to accept with serenity the things that cannot be changed, courage to change the things which should be changed, and the wisdom to distinguish the one from the other.

Author: Reinhold Niebuhr, 1934

</div>

<div class="post-lang-content" data-post-lang="zh-CN" markdown="1">

> 上帝，请赐予我平静，去接受我不能改变的；赐予我勇气，去改变我能改变的；并赐予我智慧，去分辨这两者的不同。

作者：莱因霍尔德·尼布尔（Reinhold Niebuhr），1934年

</div>
